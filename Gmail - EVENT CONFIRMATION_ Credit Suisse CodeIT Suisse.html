Event Confirmation: CodeIT Suisse September 30 – October 2

 

 

 

Congratulations, your team has been selected!

 

 

 

Dear Ching-Heng, Hong Joon, Saif and Abay,

Thank you for your application to the CodeIT Suisse coding challenge, we are pleased to announce that your team has been selected to attend it on September 30 – October 2.

Venue: Credit Suisse, Level 88, International Commerce Centre (ICC), 1 Austin Road West, Kowloon

Directions to ICC: Upon your arrival in ICC, please proceed to ICC Lobby E on Level 9 and sign up at the Credit Suisse reception (kindly allow 5 mins for this). You will then be given access to take the direct elevator to our office on Level 88. The Campus Recruiting Team will meet you at the Level 88 reception. For further information on ICC location and transportation, please visit the ICC website.

Dress code: Casual

Schedule:

All participants must bring along their own laptop/device with WiFi support for each session and instructions will be given on suggested software and programs to download. Wifi access for the laptop/device will be provided to each participant.

 

Briefing Session: to prepare you for the coding challenge, there will be a briefing session on Friday, September 30 from 5:30-7:00pm in the Credit Suisse office where all details of the coding challenge and what’s expected will be shared with you. You will also get the chance to meet other teams and also Credit Suisse’s representatives. Refreshments will be provided. Please arrive at ICC at 5:15pm for a prompt start.

Coding Challenge: the coding challenge will begin at 9:00am on Saturday, October 1. The problem statement will be revealed with Credit Suisse representatives available to guide you. Teams will be coding on-site at the Credit Suisse office from 9:00am to 6:00pm. The Project can be completed at home for the remainder of the weekend. Lunch and refreshments will be provided.

Coding Challenge and Evaluation: teams are required to return to the Credit Suisse office by 9:00am on Sunday, October 2 to complete the Project and attend the final evaluation with the CS panel. The Project must be ready by 12:00pm. Further information of the evaluation will be shared at the Friday briefing session. Lunch will be provided.

For further details on the CodeIT Suisse coding challenge, please read the Terms and Conditions attached.

Hints:

Successful participants will need to make good use of some basic technologies: JSON over HTTP, Server/Client architecture. To differentiate yourself from other teams, you can develop your skills with the following technologies: HTML, JavaScript, CSS, database CRUD operations, threading.

Though we have named a few technologies above, the use of different languages, libraries, and frameworks is encouraged!

Next Steps:

1.     We have received an overwhelming response for this event, please confirm your team’s attendance to the Friday briefing session and the coding challenge by tomorrow, September 27.

2.     We would also like to encourage you to come up with a team name, which you can share with us on Friday.

It’s going to be a fun weekend and we are very excited for it to begin. We look forward to hearing from you!
